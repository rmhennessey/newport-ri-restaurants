Best Lunch in Newport

Lobster Rolls, Clam Chowder, and Burgers üëç

When looking for "must-try" lunch options in Newport, there are some staple dishes you will want to order. >

Especially when ordering from Brick Alley Pub's Lunch Menu.

Brick Alley Pub has been a Newport landmark for over 40 years. A family owned and operated restaurant, Brick Alley almost doubles as a Newport museum. Pictures, keepsakes, and Newport memoribilia adorn the wall throughout the restaurant, giving guests both a taste of some of Newport's favorite food along with a taste of the city's history.
